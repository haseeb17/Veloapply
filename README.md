# VeloApply

VeloApply is an AI-powered job search platform that helps professionals create stronger applications, optimize resumes for ATS compatibility, generate tailored cover letters, and manage their job search from a single workspace.

Our mission is to reduce the time spent on repetitive job applications while helping candidates present their experience more effectively.

## What VeloApply Offers

- AI resume tailoring
- ATS compatibility analysis
- AI-generated cover letters
- Job application tracking
- Multiple career profiles
- PDF resume generation
- Resume scoring
- Personalized application workflows

## Who It's For

VeloApply is designed for:

- Job seekers
- Career changers
- Students and graduates
- Experienced professionals
- Recruiters and career coaches

## Repository Purpose

This repository contains public documentation, product information, and engineering resources for VeloApply.

The production application, infrastructure, and proprietary AI systems are maintained in private repositories.
